Here's Exactly What Happens When You Order

No surprises. No calls. No ambiguity. This is the full process from payment to delivery.

The Process

Every order follows the same five steps. Every time.

1

Pick Your Package

Browse the catalog on the Services page. Read each description carefully — every package lists exactly what you receive, what format it's delivered in, and how long it takes. If a package matches what you need, move to the next step.

2

Complete Payment

Click "Order This Package" on any card. You'll go to the secure Stripe checkout. Payment is processed immediately and you'll receive a Stripe confirmation email. No account creation required.

3

Complete the Intake Form

After payment, you'll be redirected to a short intake form. Answer every question honestly and thoroughly — the quality of your deliverable depends on the quality of your intake. The delivery clock starts the moment your completed form is received. Incomplete forms delay delivery.

4

Work Is Produced

Your deliverable is produced using AI tools guided by a human. The prompting, structuring, editing, and quality review are all done by a real person. You're not getting raw AI output. Every deliverable is checked against the agreed scope before it leaves.

5

Delivery and Revision

Your finished deliverable arrives in your inbox as a Google Doc (or the format stated in your package) within the delivery window. Every package includes one round of revisions — submit one consolidated set of notes and updates come back within 24 hours.

What You Need to Provide

The intake form covers all of this. Have these ready before you fill it out.

A clear description of your business and what you sell
Your target audience and who you're writing for
Any examples of your existing tone or brand voice
Specific goals for the deliverable — what it needs to accomplish
Any platforms, formats, or technical requirements
The email address where you want the deliverable sent

Common Questions

Answers to what most people ask before they order.

Is this AI-generated content, or is a human writing it?
Both. Every deliverable is produced using AI tools guided by a human — the prompting, structuring, editing, and quality review are done by a real person. You're not getting raw AI output.
What if I need revisions?
Every package includes one round of revisions. Submit one consolidated set of revision notes and we'll update the work within 24 hours. Additional rounds are $49 each.
Do I get to talk to someone before I buy?
No discovery calls. Each package has a clear description of what you get. If you have a specific question, reach out via the contact form — we respond within one business day.
What if I'm not happy with what I receive?
Use your included revision round. If the deliverable genuinely doesn't match the agreed scope, contact us and we'll make it right.
How fast is the turnaround, really?
The stated timeframe is a commitment, not an estimate. The clock starts when your completed intake form is received.
What format do I receive my deliverable in?
Most deliverables are Google Docs. Some structured documents may be delivered as PDFs. Format is confirmed in your intake confirmation email.
Can I order more than one package at a time?
Yes. Each package is processed independently with its own intake form and delivery window.
What if I'm not sure which package is right for me?
Read the descriptions carefully. If still unsure, use the contact form to describe what you're trying to accomplish.
