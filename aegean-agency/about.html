Why I Built This

Most solopreneurs and small business owners don't need a full agency retainer. They need one thing done well, on a deadline, at a price that makes sense. The problem is the industry is built around the opposite: discovery calls, proposals, hourly billing, and ongoing relationships that cost more than they're worth.

I built aegeanAI around a simple premise: every common business writing and content problem has a repeatable solution. The combination of AI tooling and structured human review makes it possible to deliver that solution faster and cheaper than a traditional freelancer — without sacrificing quality.

Every package in the catalog is a problem I've watched business owners solve badly — by ignoring it, by doing it themselves at the wrong time, or by paying too much for something that didn't come back right. These packages exist because the problems are real and the solutions are replicable.

The goal is simple: you pick what you need, you fill out the form, you get it back. No meetings. No back-and-forth. No surprises.
