## Scope-Creep Risk Register

The following packages carry elevated scope risk and require strict intake gating:

| Package | Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| 03 — AI SOPs in a Day | Client provides vague process descriptions requiring multiple clarification rounds | Require written process description OR Loom before work begins |
| 08 — VoC Research Synthesis | Insufficient or low-quality data from client | Minimum 20 data points required; decline if not met |
| 10 — AI Chatbot Script | Client expects chatbot setup/integration, not just script | Explicit disclaimer in offer copy and confirmation email |
| 13 — Notion CRM Setup | Client requests automations or integrations beyond native Notion | Hard scope boundary in offer: Notion-native only |
| 16 — Website Copy (5 Pages) | Clients request revisions, additional pages, or dev handoff | One revision round included; additional pages are add-on SKUs |
| 12 — Sales Script | High creative judgment required; revision-prone | Build in 30-min buffer; deliver with brief rationale doc |

**Packages to Consider Cutting from V1**: Package 12 (Sales Script) and Package 16 (Website Copy) are the highest fulfillment complexity. Both are fulfillable, but they are the most prone to revision requests and creative disagreements. Recommend adding them at Week 2 after intake form and delivery process are validated.
