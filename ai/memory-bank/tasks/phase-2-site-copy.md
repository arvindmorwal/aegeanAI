## The Full Process

### Step 1: Choose Your Package and Pay
Browse the catalog, pick the deliverable you need, and complete checkout. Payment is processed securely through Stripe. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately after purchase.

### Step 2: Fill Out the Intake Form
Your confirmation email includes a link to a short intake form. This is where you give us everything we need to do the work — your business info, target audience, offer details, tone preferences, and any specific instructions. Most clients complete this in under 10 minutes.

**The quality of your deliverable depends directly on the quality of your intake answers. The more specific you are, the better the output.**

### Step 3: We Build Your Deliverable
Once your intake form is submitted, work begins within a few hours. Every deliverable is built specifically for your business using your inputs — not pulled from a generic template bank.

### Step 4: Delivery to Your Inbox
Your finished deliverable arrives within the stated turnaround window. The clock starts when your intake form is received, not when you purchased.

### Step 5: Review and Request Revisions (If Needed)
If something doesn't land right, you have one revision round. Submit your revision notes and the updated version comes back within 24 hours.

- Your business name and what you do
- Your target audience — who you serve and what they struggle with
- Your offer or product — what you sell and how it works

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is this AI-generated content, or is a human writing it?**
Both. Every deliverable is produced using AI tools guided by a human — the prompting, structuring, editing, and quality review are done by a real person who understands copywriting and content strategy. You're not getting raw AI output.

**What if I need revisions?**
Every package includes one round of revisions. Submit one consolidated set of revision notes and we'll update the work within 24 hours. Additional revision rounds are available for $49 per round.

**Do I get to talk to someone before I buy?**
No discovery calls. The catalog is self-explanatory. If you have a specific question, reach out via the contact form and we'll respond within one business day.

**What if I'm not happy with what I receive?**
Use your included revision round. If the deliverable genuinely doesn't match the agreed scope, contact us and we'll make it right.

**How fast is the turnaround, really?**
The stated timeframe (24, 48, or 72 hours) is a commitment. The clock starts when your completed intake form is received.

**What format do I receive my deliverable in?**
Most deliverables are Google Docs. Some structured documents may be PDFs. Format is confirmed in your intake confirmation email.

**Can I order more than one package at a time?**
Yes. Each package is processed independently with its own intake form and delivery window.

**What if I'm not sure which package is right for me?**
Read the descriptions carefully. If still unsure, use the contact form to describe what you're trying to accomplish.
